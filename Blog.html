The Centre for Research and Knowledge Dissemination (CRKD) of Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka is planning to hold the 9th edition of its biennial International Conference (9th International Conference of Sabaragamuwa University of Sri Lanka - ICSUSL 2023) on 6-8 December 2023 under the theme of "Advancing Multidisciplinary Approaches for a Sustainable and Resilient Future".


With the rising energy prices and inflation across the globe, declining food supply, soaring world population, unwarranted political conflicts, culture wars, and major environmental challenges, we are at the crossroads. The present demands actions to reverse course and find sustainable solutions to save humanity and nature from the brink of total collapse. With eight tracks, the ICSUSL 2023 yields a platform for scholars and scientific minds worldwide to create global partnerships to achieve the Sustainable Development Goals set by the United Nations and lead the movement by advancing multidisciplinary approaches for a sustainable and resilient future
